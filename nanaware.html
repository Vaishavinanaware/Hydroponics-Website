Full Nutrient Deficiency Reference

  • Nitrogen (N): Pale/light green leaves, lower leaves yellow → Add nitrogen source (e.g., Calcium Nitrate)
  • Phosphorus (P): Dark/ purple tinge on leaves, poor growth → Add MKP
  • Potassium (K): Brown edges/tip burn → Potassium Sulphate
  • Calcium (Ca): Deformed new leaves / tip burn → Calcium Nitrate
  • Magnesium (Mg): Interveinal yellowing → Epsom salt (MgSO₄)
  • Iron (Fe): New leaves yellow with green veins → Iron chelate
  • Micronutrients (Zn, Mn, B, Cu, Mo): Stunting, spots, poor fruit set → Use a complete micronutrient mix
